Minecraft

Minecraft is a game I've dedicated many hours to playing. It's allowed me to be able to express creativity in a simple and fun way. For over 13 years, I've been building and creating in Minecraft. For 3 years, I've been working on a city complete with highways, suspension bridges, and skyscrapers.

ULTRAKILL

ULTRAKILL is a fast-paced first-person shooter that combines elements of classic shooters with modern mechanics. The game has a high skill ceiling and encourages players to speedrun the game as fast as possible. I enjoy the fast paced gameplay and the unique combat mechanics, and enjoy the challenges available in each level. My favorite weapon in the game is the Marksman, a pistol that shoots coins that can ricochet bullets to enemies.

Terraria

Terraria is a 2D sandbox game that allows players to explore, build, and fight in a procedurally generated world. I enjoy the reward of defeating powerful bosses and growing my character through the game. I have beat the game on multiple difficulties, but my favorite is in the Calamity mod, a large addition to the game that adds bosses, items and includes some of my favorite content.

Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley is a calm farming simulator that lets players grow crops on their farm, raise animals, and live off the land. I enjoy its cozy atmosphere and the relaxing gameplay, and being an Indie Game, it inspired me to try to create my own games. It also inspired some of my artwork.
